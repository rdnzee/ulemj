About SkyServe

SkyServe Catering specializes in premium in-flight catering solutions for commercial and private airlines. We operate 24/7 from major airport hubs and deliver fresh, high-quality meals tailored to various dietary needs and cultural preferences.

With over 15 years in the aviation catering industry, we understand the importance of punctuality, hygiene, and excellence.

  • Special dietary meals: vegetarian, kosher, halal, gluten-free
  • 24/7 logistics and on-time delivery to aircraft
  • VIP and private jet catering
  • Custom branding and airline-specific menu design
  • Waste management and eco-friendly packaging solutions
